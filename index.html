ChickenBot is my fabulous discord bot. It's one of my rare successful projects. It's a multi-purpose discord bot. Mainly for personal usage to make life easier. But in the latest version I have made it more user-friendly.

It can play music, show cute cat pictures, do some basic moderation, log stuff or remind you about something. You can also use it's many utilities like polls, userinfo and message quotation. It even has a xp based activity with levels and leaderboard. (role rewards on levels coming soon I hope)

In future when I have time and mind I'll try to make a web based easy to use dashboard and add tons of new features.

ChickenBot is hosted with node.js (v14) using the discord.js (v12) library on my vps.
